The Big Idea

Expanding into a new market isn’t just about reaching new customers; it’s about doing it the right way. By taking the time to reassess pet insurance pricing based on breed-specific claim trends, we can optimize premiums to attract more customers and stay competitive in the European market, while failing to do so risks losing market share to better-priced alternatives.

The Path Forward

By optimizing our premiums based on data-driven insights, we will:

  • Attract More Customers: Offering competitive pricing ensures that pet owners see BeanSprouts as the go-to choice for affordable and reliable coverage.
  • Stay Ahead of Competitors: A thoughtful approach to pricing safeguards market share and positions us as a leader in the European pet insurance market.
  • Maintain High Standards: We’ll uphold the same quality and dependability that our U.S. customers have come to expect.
